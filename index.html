Arroz Chaufa

Arroz chaufa also known as Arroz de chaufa is a Chinese-fried rice dish. It is a chifa style dish, a Chinese cuisine. It consists of a mix of fried rice with vegetables, usually including scallions, eggs, and chicken, quickly cooked at a high flame, often in a wok with soy sauce and oil.
